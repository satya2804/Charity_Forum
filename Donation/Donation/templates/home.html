About US

CHARITY FORUM

Charity Forum is a centralized platform that accepts donations and distributes them to a variety of different organizations with which we have connected to. This website easily lets people make In-Kind donations such as redistributing surplus food, water, clothing, books, and medical supplies to individuals who are in need.
The Vision of The Charity Forum:
Our Vision is to provide a platform that enables individuals and organizations to create positive change in their communities. Our platform connects people with the charities they care about and provides them with the tools they need to make a positive impact.
